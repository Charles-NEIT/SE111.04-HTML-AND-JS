Review

Charles O'Connell

4/17/2025

Before strating Doctor Strange (2016) is my favorite MCU marvel movie it has the best cinematography and special effects out of any marvel movie even today. This marvel movie continued the greatness of the phase of marvel movies at the time will still maintaining an independent story that could be watched without the marvel context to it. However, this movie leads nicely into all the future marvel projects. Benedict Cumberbatch's performance as Doctor Stephan Strange is amazing and very natural.


Within the movie there is a great story told about a doctor recovering and finding a magic that is not from his world. This movie will have you with its amazing visuals amazing storytelling and characters will still maintaining comic relief at points. Doctor Strange still remains the best marvel movie in my opinion and I believe everyone must watch it. I would this movie a must watch 9.4 out of 10.
