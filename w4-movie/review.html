Review

Doctor Strange (2016) is a movie in which a well skilled surgeon (Doctor Stephan Strange) goes through a terrible accident and loses control of his hands. When his hands fail him he undergoes anything possible to recover his hands strength. When he cant resort to anything else and all hope is lost he is told about an experimental place that could help him regain his hands control. This place he goes to is mysterious showing him a magic that he cant believe with his eyes. He later becomes a student that eventually becomes well versed in this magic and becomes a master or the sorcerer supreme and has to fight against the dark magic being known as dormammu. This movie is part of the marvel cinematic universe and Dr Strange is an essential piece in it.

This movie was produced by marvel studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures (Worldwide). It released in November 4, 2016. The genre is Action, superhero,adventure, fantasy, and science Fiction. This movie is pg-13 with 7.5 imdb rating and has runtime of 1 hour 55 minutes.
